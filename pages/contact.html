Biologia

Located in the heart of Playa Grande, SurfShack Biologia is the venue of choice for most of the surfing events in Argentina. Playa grande has 2 of the best beach breaks you will find. In addition, SurfShack has all the gear you need for classes, and if you want to buy new gear.

Mole

Located in the famous praia mole, SurfShack Mole is our biggest store with a wide variety of boards and wetsuits. Also our most busy store as its open year round and provides classes from beginner level to expert.

Lobitos

Our newest addition to the SurfShack brand is the Lobitos store. SurfShack Lobitos is situated by the powerful pacific surf. Most our experience students like this location as it helps them boost their technical skills thanks to our world class coaches and world class waves. In addition, you will find all the latest gear from the best brands in the market.
