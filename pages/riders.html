GRIFFIN COLAPINTO

Griffin Colapinto is an American professional surfer from California. In 2017, Griffin won the prestigious Vans Triple Crown event. That same year, he also won the Men's Qualifying Series and qualified for the WSL Tour.

GABRIEL MEDINA

Gabriel Medina is a Brazilian professional surfer who won the 2014, 2018 and 2021 WSL World Championships. With 17 WSL Championship Tour event wins and 29 Final appearances, Medina is one of the most experienced surfers on tour.

JOHN JOHN
FLORENCE

"John John" Florence is a Hawaiian professional surfer best known as one of the most dominant pipe surfers of his era. He won back-to-back world titles in 2016 and 2017, and was the first to do it since the late Andy Irons.
